Research:

Revised manuscript in Sci Zone 3 PLOS ONE: "Research on a Financial Fraud Identification Model by Fusing a Convolutional Neural Network."

  • Proposed a financial fraud identification model that combines Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and Support Vector Machines (SVM)
  • Visualization of feature contributions, feature correlation analysis.
  • Compared the performance of CNN-SVM with LR, RF, XGBoost, and SVM, and perform statistical testing

The submitted manuscript: "Shareholder Networks, Information Advantages, and Corporate Digital Transformation."

  • Studied the impact of shareholder relationship networks on digital transformation of Chinese listed companies.
  • Found that these networks positively influenced digital transformation through "information advantages," especially among stable institutional investors.
  • Weakened the effect in state-owned enterprises due to policy burdens, but compensation incentives strengthened it.
  • Identified that economic policy uncertainty and marketization levels affected network value, with stronger effects in non-high-tech firms and eastern regions.

The submitted manuscript："Assessment of the Effectiveness and Impact of Green Agricultural Policies: A Quantitative Analysis Based on Policy Texts from China (2017–2023)"

  • Conducted a quantitative analysis of China's green agricultural policies from the perspective of policy texts.
  • Derived indicators from the analysis as independent variables, with the SBM-GML index used to measure Agricultural Green Total Factor Productivity (AGTFP) as the dependent variable.
  • Established a multiple regression model for empirical analysis of policy implementation effectiveness.
  • Found that supply-side policy tools had a significant positive effect on agricultural green total factor productivity.
  • Revealed that environmental policy tools had a significant negative impact on agricultural green total factor productivity.

The submitted manuscript："Assessment of the Effectiveness and Impact of Photovoltaic Industry Policies: A Quantitative Analysis Based on Policy Texts from China (2005–2023)"

  • Quantified and analyzed policies related to China's photovoltaic industry.
  • Used the SBM-GML model to measure Green Total Factor Productivity (GTFP).
  • Conducted empirical analysis on the impact of policy tools on GTFP.
  • Found that both supply-side and demand-side policy tools had a significant positive effect on Green Total Factor Productivity.

Ongoing manuscript: "The Application of Media Coverage in Financial Distress Warning—Based on Bayesian Optimized LightGBM Model Construction and Empirical Research."

  • Analyzed media reports on Chinese A-share listed companies using text mining and NLP techniques.
  • Optimized LightGBM parameters with Bayesian methods to construct a financial fraud warning model.
  • Found that Bayesian optimization significantly reduced the model's error rate.
  • Identified negative media coverage as significantly related to financial distress warnings, considering biases and sentiment.
